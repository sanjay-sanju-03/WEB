About Us

LBS College of Engineering Kasaragod, a college that stood up for its vision and excellence in education established in the year 1993 under the LBS Center for Science and Technology, Thiruvananthapuram, has held its pride and power as the most prestigious and highly facilitated college with unbeatable academic strength across the various streams of engineering. With innovations and outstanding performance, the Association of IT keeps its strength unmatched. CIPHER is an annual technical fest organized by the IT association to showcase the latest technologies, innovations, and ideas from around the world. This year ' s Techfest promises to be bigger and better than ever before, with an array of exciting events and competitions lined up. This national-level technical fest organized by the Association of IT is one of South India ' s largest TechnoCultural Festivals
